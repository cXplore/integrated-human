## AI VERIFICATION SYSTEM

Every learning path could include:

### 1. Journal Quality Evaluation
- AI reads entries
- Scores: Depth, Specificity, Self-Reflection
- Rating: Pass / Needs Depth / Try Again

### 2. Pattern Recognition Verification
- AI checks pattern maps for specificity
- "This is too vague - add specific examples"

### 3. Skill Demonstration Scoring
- Scenarios presented
- User responds
- AI evaluates against rubric (repair, boundary, conflict)

### 4. Progress Gates
- Can't proceed until AI approves previous work
- Not just completion - quality threshold

### 5. Conversation Simulation
- AI plays partner/scenario
- Practice repair, difficult conversations
- Real-time feedback

### 6. Final Assessment
- AI presents 3 scenarios
- User demonstrates skills
- Must hit threshold to pass

**Result: Certificates mean demonstrated competence, not just completion.**
