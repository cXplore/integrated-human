---

## What Sovereignty Means

### It Is:
- Self-governance—making choices from your center
- Owning your power without apology
- Knowing your worth as inherent
- Being the author of your life
- Wholeness, not perfection

### It Is Not:
- Never needing anyone
- Being invulnerable
- Having it all figured out
- Hardness or walls
- Doing it all alone

### The Sovereign Woman:
Makes choices from her center, not from others' expectations. Knows her worth. Owns her power. Stays connected to herself while connecting to others.
